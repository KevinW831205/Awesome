Awesome Word Game

      Click on your game mode to see the instructions:

    • Definition Mode
      Read the definition given, and click one of the four words in the table which matches the definition. If you get the right answer, you get one point. You will be given a total of 10 definitions.
    • Synonym Mode
      You will be given a random word; choose which of the four words in the table is the synonym for the given word. If you get the right answer, you get one point. You will be given a total of 10 random words.
    • Antonym Mode
      You will be given a random word; choose which of the four words in the table is the antonym for the given word. If you get the right answer, you get one point. You will be given a total of 10 random words.
